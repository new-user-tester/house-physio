Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about physiotherapy

What should I expect during my first physiotherapy session?

Your first session will include a comprehensive assessment of your condition, medical history review, physical examination, and discussion of treatment goals. We'll develop a personalized treatment plan and may begin initial therapy techniques.

How long does a typical physiotherapy session last?

Most sessions last between 45-60 minutes, depending on your specific condition and treatment plan. Initial assessments may take longer, up to 75 minutes.

Do I need a doctor's referral to see a physiotherapist?

In most cases, you don't need a referral to see a physiotherapist. However, some insurance plans may require a doctor's referral for coverage. We recommend checking with your insurance provider beforehand.

How many physiotherapy sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your condition, its severity, and how your body responds to treatment. After your initial assessment, your physiotherapist will provide an estimated treatment timeline.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our office with your insurance details, and we'll verify your coverage before your appointment.
